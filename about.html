Our Story in Dauin

La Tortue was created to share the house reef, critters, and relaxed island life of Dauin. From a handful of rooms we grew into a seaside hideaway with bamboo-style cottages, an open-air restaurant, and a dedicated dive center right on the beach. Every stay is personal, every dive is guided with care, and every guest is treated like family.

SSI Training

Beginner to pro courses with flexible pacing, calm entry points, and nitrox available.

Small Groups

House reef macro, Dauin muck dives, and Apo Island turtles—kept to intimate groups for more bottom time.

Beachfront Base

Cottages, rinse and camera areas, gear storage, and a chill restaurant and bar steps from the water.

How We Dive

We focus on relaxed, safe, and quiet dives—whether you’re trying scuba for the first time, leveling up with Advanced, or joining a macro hunt before sunrise.

House Reef & Muck

Critters at the doorstep: frogfish, octopus species, ghost pipefish, and turtles at sunset.

Apo Island Days

Private banca, limited spots, and drift dives over coral gardens with turtles on every trip.

Photo-Friendly

Camera rinse and tables, patient spotting, and guides who know where the residents like to hide.

Comfort First

Modern rental gear, nitrox 32%, warm briefings, and schedules that fit your travel rhythm.

The Resort

Wake up to the sound of the sea, warm coffee by the sand, and sunsets behind the palm trees. We keep the footprint small and the atmosphere easy—just how Dauin should feel.

Bamboo & Garden Cottages

Airy rooms with AC, balconies, and plenty of storage for wet and dry gear.

Beach Dining

Breakfast staples, Filipino favorites, pastas and burgers, shakes and cocktails by the shore.

Easy Transfers

Close to Sibulan airport and the Dumaguete pier—ferries to Siquijor, Bohol, and Cebu within reach.
