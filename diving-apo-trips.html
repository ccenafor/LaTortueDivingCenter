Apo Island Day Trips

Apo Island is a volcanic island surrounded by over 400 species of corals and a diverse marine life, including big schools of fish and over 250 recorded resident sea turtles.

Join us for a full day trip to one of the most sought-after diving destinations in the Philippines!

Apo Island Highlights

Swap Dauin's black sand for blazing coral walls and drifting pelagics. These are the sites we rotate through depending on current, experience, and mood.

Apo Island

Rockpoint East / West

Recommended: All levels  |  Depth: 5–25 m

Gentle southern slopes blanketed in hard corals and schooling reef life.

  • Marine life: Green and hawksbill turtles, clouds of anthias, banded sea kraits.
  • Why we love it: Shallow coral gardens perfect for photographers.
Apo Island

Chapel

Recommended: All levels  |  Depth: 5–40 m

A gentle reef that transitions into a dramatic wall beneath the hillside chapel.

  • Marine life: Turtles, fusiliers, batfish, barracudas, electric clams, frogfish, nudibranchs.
  • Why we love it: Dreamy Deep Specialty finale with a tiny cavern.
Apo Island

Coconut Point

Recommended: Experienced  |  Depth: 10–25 m

Locals call it "The Washing Machine" thanks to exhilarating multi-directional currents.

  • Marine life: Tuna, jacks, barracudas, fusiliers, emperor angelfish, turtles.
  • Why we love it: A rollercoaster drift dive for current junkies.
Apo Island

Kan-uran

Recommended: All levels  |  Depth: 5–25 m

Southeast reef combining shallow shelves with a relaxed slope.

  • Marine life: Green and hawksbill turtles, tropical reef species, nudibranchs.
  • Why we love it: Calm, colorful, and camera-friendly.
Apo Island

Largahan

Recommended: All levels  |  Depth: 5–20 m

Volcanic bubbles escape the sand, creating a sparkling underwater hot spring.

  • Marine life: Turtles, nudibranchs, clouds of reef fish.
  • Why we love it: A natural jacuzzi for divers.
Apo Island

Cogon

Recommended: Intermediate (Advanced required)  |  Depth: 8–30 m

Current-swept slope lined with soft and hard corals plus towering gorgonians.

  • Marine life: Turtles, tuna, barracudas, jacks, snappers, rainbow runners, spotted mackerel.
  • Why we love it: Wall-to-wall schooling action.
Apo Island

Katipanan

Recommended: All levels  |  Depth: 5–18 m

White sand, soft corals, and mellow currents — a relaxed finale dive.

  • Marine life: Turtles in snorkel-depth water, reef fish, banded sea kraits.
  • Why we love it: A serene way to end the day.
