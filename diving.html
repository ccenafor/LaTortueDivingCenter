Who hasn’t dreamed of turning their passion into a career by teaching and inspiring divers from around the world while living in sync with the sea?

The SSI Instructor Training Course is the next step into becoming a true educator and leader in the professional diving world.

This course goes far beyond learning how to teach from a manual. Over two intensive weeks, you’ll be fully immersed in the instructor mindset, working closely with our team through workshops, simulations, and practical teaching exercises. You’ll practice confined water, open water, and classroom presentations in realistic scenarios designed to prepare you for real-world teaching.

Through hands-on training, role-playing, and mentorship, you’ll refine your demonstration quality, presentation skills, and problem-solving abilities. Level 1 - Supervised diver to 20m (PE20)
3 days • Min age 12 • ₱19,500

Level 1 is the first diving certification. This complete course gives you the skills and confidence needed to dive to 20 meters, anywhere in the world.

FFESSM Level 1 is very similar to SSI Open Water Diver. However, Level 1 does not require a final exam and includes fewer open-water exercises.

At La Tortue, we make this training both serious, friendly, and inspiring, in a safe and supportive setting. Each session is designed to match your pace in an ocean-respectful environment.

What to expect:

  • Theory: sessions with the instructor and PDF course materials provided
  • 6 sea dives in Dauin's protected marine areas to build skills, work on buoyancy, and enjoy the underwater world safely.

Our sites offer ideal training conditions: accessible, shallow reefs, abundant marine life, and moderate currents.
