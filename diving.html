Try Scuba
• 2.5 hours • Min age 8 • ₱3,000

Gentle, instructor-led intro in calm water. Short briefing on gear and hand signals, then a guided descent to meet our house-reef critters.

Loved it? This dive can count as the first session of your Open Water course.

Open Water Diver

Your first certification to 18m. Serious yet fun and supportive, paced to you, with ocean respect at the core.
