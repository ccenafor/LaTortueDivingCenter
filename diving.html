Try Scuba
• 2.5 hours • Min age 8 • ₱3,000

Have you been yearning to experience scuba diving? Our Try Scuba experience is a gentle introduction led by experienced instructors in an exceptional marine environment.

What to expect:

  • A short classroom session to learn the basics and discover how to use your dive gear
  • Easy, shore entry
  • Basic safety exercises in shallow water
  • Reef exploration to admire coral and marine life

Dauin's marine sanctuaries are teeming with life: colorful coral, tropical fish (clownfish, parrotfish, surgeonfish, butterflyfish, angelfish, and batfish), and both green and hawksbill turtles are frequently seen.

Duration: approx. 2.5 hours including the classroom session
Minimum age: 8
Experience: No prior dive experience required
Language: French or English
Price: ₱3,000

Loved the experience? Continue with us and get discounted additional dives or start your Open Water Diver course—your Try Scuba dive can count as your first dive!

Perfect Buoyancy / Refresher
2.5–3 hours • ₱2,500

Refine your buoyancy to better control your movements, kick more smoothly, and reduce your air consumption—all while protecting marine life. At La Tortue Diving, we adapt to your diving style, whether you're a beginner or already experienced.

What to expect (Perfect Buoyancy):

  • A theory session to set your improvement goals
  • Exercises in a controlled environment to fine-tune buoyancy
  • Games to improve your trim and positioning
  • Personalized tips on weighting and breathing
  • 2 dives to put your progress into practice

Dauin's dive sites are perfect for this specialty: gradual depth, abundant marine life, and little current.

Duration: 1 day • Minimum age: 10 • Prerequisite: Open Water Diver (or equivalent) • Price: ₱9,800

Need a refresher instead? The Refresher is a gentle, personalized update to help you get back underwater with peace of mind.

  • 100% personalized guidance based on your needs
  • Theory review and refresher on golden safety rules
  • Skill review: mask clearing, buoyancy, equipment setup, air sharing, and an easy dive
  • Relaxed, pressure-free atmosphere to regain comfort underwater

Duration: 2.5–3 hours • Minimum age: 10 • Prerequisite: Open Water Diver (or equivalent) • Recommended: if you haven't dived in 6+ months • Price: ₱2,500

Divemaster (Pro)

Who hasn't dreamed of sharing their passion by guiding divers from around the world while living to the rhythm of the ocean? The Divemaster course is your gateway into the professional world of scuba diving.

We offer an immersive and comprehensive experience that goes far beyond a theoretical program. You'll become part of our team and gain hands-on experience through regular diving.
