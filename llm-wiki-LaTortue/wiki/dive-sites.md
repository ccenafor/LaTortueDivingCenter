The dive-sites page positions Dauin as a coastline of dark volcanic sand, marine protected areas, shore-entry coastal dives, and boat-access Apo Island trips (source: diving-sites.html; source: fr/diving-sites.html). The page includes a map image for the Dauin coast and Apo Island dive sites near La Tortue (source: diving-sites.html; source: fr/diving-sites.html).

## House Reef And Sanctuary Circuit

The "House Reef & Sanctuary Circuit" section includes La Tortue House Reef, Poblacion District 1, El Dorado, Masaplod Norte, Kagamas, Talisay 2 / Sea Dream, Poblacion District 2, Silver Reef, and Mainit (source: diving-sites.html; source: fr/diving-sites.html).

The La Tortue House Reef is recommended for all levels at 2-20 m and highlights clownfish, parrotfish, trumpetfish, nudibranchs, shrimp, crabs, plus resident green and hawksbill turtles (source: diving-sites.html). El Dorado is listed as all levels with Advanced preferred at 5-25 m and is described as strong for fish volume, size, and variety (source: diving-sites.html).

## Muck And Macro Playground

The "Muck & Macro Playground" section includes Talisay, Pyramid, Malatapay, La Tortue Muck Site, Sahara Deep, San Miguel, Bonnet Corner, Thalatta, and Alura (source: diving-sites.html; source: fr/diving-sites.html).

Malatapay is presented as a go-to night option with ghost pipefish, frogfish, shrimp, crabs, nudibranchs, octopus, squid, and cuttlefish (source: diving-sites.html). Bonnet Corner is presented as prime octopus territory, including mimic, wunderpus, coconut, mototi, blue-ringed, and algae octopus (source: diving-sites.html).

## Apo Island Sites

The dedicated Apo Island page lists Rockpoint East/West, Chapel, Coconut Point, Kan-uran, Largahan, Cogon, and Katipanan as Apo highlights (source: diving-apo-trips.html; source: fr/diving-apo-trips.html).
